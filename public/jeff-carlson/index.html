What I'd Build

A System That Works Like You Think

This is the same AI operating system I build for superintendents and business leaders. Configured to how you actually work.

Intelligent Email Triage

Your AI scans all three inboxes and sorts by what actually matters. Partnership replies surface immediately. Receipts get filed. Spam gets flagged before you waste a Friday morning on it. You open your laptop and see what needs you, not what found you.

Draft Engine

You tell it what to say. It drafts in your voice, with the right tone for the right audience: formal for a state department, warm for a district partner, quick for an internal thread. You review, adjust, send. The writing time drops from 30 minutes to 3.

Partnership Memory

One file per partner, district, and vendor. Every conversation, every commitment, every follow-up: captured and searchable. Before a call with a state ed agency, your AI pulls up the full history so you walk in with context, not guesses.

Travel Mode

On a plane with no WiFi? Brain dump everything into your system: meeting notes, follow-ups, email drafts. When you land, it's all organized, scheduled, and ready to send at the right time. Your flight time becomes your most productive time without the 10 PM replies.

Receipt and Purchase Routing

That credit card email problem you described? Solved. Purchases, subscriptions, and receipts get automatically tagged and filed. They never touch your main view. Check them when you want, ignore them when you don't.
