📄 Case Description: NovaBrew International

NovaBrew International is a $48M specialty beverage company headquartered in Austin, Texas. Founded eight years ago, the company has grown from a regional cold brew brand to a nationally recognized player in the premium beverage space, with products available in over 1,200 U.S. retail locations including Whole Foods, Sprouts, and Target.

The company operates three product lines: NovaBrew Cold Brew (flagship, 55% of revenue), NovaBoost functional drinks with adaptogens (30%, fastest growing), and NovaSpark sparkling botanicals (15%). All products are non-GMO, contain no artificial sweeteners, and use sustainably sourced ingredients. Production is centralized in Buda, TX.

After achieving 33% year-over-year revenue growth and securing $7M in net profit, NovaBrew's board of directors has approved a $12 million international expansion budget over 24 months. The company has zero international presence. Domestic growth is slowing (from 35% to 18% YoY), and three competitors have already entered European and Asian markets.

Your assignment: As a consulting team, conduct a comprehensive analysis of NovaBrew's strategic position and develop a recommendation for international expansion. You will work through 8 modules covering industry analysis, competitive positioning, business model evaluation, market selection, and strategic planning. Use the company executives (CEO, VP Marketing, VP Operations) as resources, but remember: they will guide your thinking, not hand you the answers.

Key data: Revenue $48M | Employees 240 | Gross Margin 52% | Net Margin 14.5% | Expansion Budget $12M | U.S. Retail 1,200+ | International Markets 0 | Three target markets under consideration: Brazil, South Korea, United Kingdom.
