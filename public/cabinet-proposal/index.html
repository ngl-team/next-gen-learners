Section 5 · How delivery works

One hundred percent virtual. No credentials change hands.

I never log into your M365 tenant. Every action happens through one of five mechanisms below. The cybersecurity-paranoid default, made operational.

1. You drive, I instruct

You stay logged in. I walk you through every click via Zoom or Teams. You hit save. Most native config (Outlook, Loop, Planner, Bookings, SharePoint) ships this way.

2. Temporary mouse handoff

You grant me mouse control via Zoom remote-control. You revoke with one click. I never see a credential. Used for fiddly multi-step builds.

3. Pre-built imports

I build Power Automate flows, agents, and SharePoint templates on my own dev tenant. Export as solution files. You import inside yours.

4. Loom walkthrough + async

5-min video of me doing it on my dev tenant. You follow on your own time. Lower friction for simple repeatable config.

5. Written spec + checklist

1-page doc you replicate inside your tenant. Used for folder taxonomies, naming conventions, list schemas.

Every session screen-recorded

You keep the recording. Replay any session, any time, forever. Loom links saved to your SharePoint Prompt Library alongside the prompts that built each deliverable.

Your tenant. Your credentials. Your control. End to end. The cybersecurity-paranoid default.
