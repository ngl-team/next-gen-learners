Beyond Time

Capabilities You Don't Have Today

These aren't faster versions of what you already do. These are things that are currently impossible — no matter how many hours you work.

Institutional Memory

Every meeting, decision, and conversation — captured, searchable, and connected. When someone asks "didn't we discuss this last October?" you have the answer in seconds. Not in your head. In the system.

Personalized Communication at Scale

Draft 30 unique parent emails about 30 different students, each referencing specific context. Send thoughtful follow-ups to every teacher after an observation. Currently impossible by hand.

Decisions with Full Context

Before responding to any email or walking into any meeting, know the complete history: what was discussed, what was decided, what's still open. Your AI holds the context so you make the right call, not just a fast one.
