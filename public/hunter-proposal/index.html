1. Scope of Work

Next Generation Learners ("Provider") will deliver and maintain a custom lead-generation system ("Hunter") built around the Client's manual real-estate prospecting workflow, including:

  1. MLS Pull & Filter - Automated Pinergy MLS scrape of expired, canceled, and withdrawn listings within a 20-mile radius of Wellesley, MA, 18-month lookback, across multi-family, land, commercial, and business opportunity property types.
  2. Active Cross-Reference - Automatic suppression of any listings that have since relisted active, ensuring Client is not contacting sellers about properties already back on market.
  3. Owner Resolution - Public-records ownership lookup including LLC-to-manager chain resolution via Massachusetts Corporation Search.
  4. Skip Trace Integration - Spokeo integration to retrieve owner phone, email, mailing address, and family network (children, grandchildren, siblings, parents). Spokeo credentials provided by Client.
  5. Pipeline & Outreach Tracking - Seven-stage kanban pipeline with timestamped stage history, automatic outreach logging (calls, voicemails, emails), and auto-advancement of pipeline stages based on outreach result.
  6. Export & Refresh - Google Sheets export (CSV and live API) and automatic refresh on a two-week cadence.
  7. Maintenance - Ongoing scraper maintenance, bug fixes, and feature requests for the duration of the maintenance retainer.

5. Data Privacy & Security

Provider takes Client-data confidentiality seriously. Key commitments:

  1. Hunter operates on Provider-managed infrastructure; access is limited to Provider and Client.
  2. Spokeo and Pinergy credentials provided by Client are stored securely and used solely for the operation of Hunter on Client's behalf.
  3. Provider will not access, copy, or share Client lead data with third parties.
  4. Upon termination, Client retains full export of all pipeline data. Provider deletes Client credentials within 7 days of termination.

6. Intellectual Property

All lead data, pipeline records, outreach logs, and exported spreadsheets generated within Hunter belong to Client. The underlying Hunter system architecture, scraping methodology, and code remain the intellectual property of Next Generation Learners and may be adapted for use with other clients.
