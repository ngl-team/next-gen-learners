Activity

The Human
Robot Game

I'm a robot now. I can only do
exactly what you tell me. Nothing more.

Round 1 - Drawing

Tell me what to draw.

Rule 1
You describe, I draw
Pick anything - a house, a car, a character, an animal. You tell me what to draw out loud.
Rule 2
I only do what you say
"Draw a house" = a square with a triangle on top. No windows. No door. No color. Unless you say so.
Example instructions:

"Pick up the pencil with your right hand. Hold it like you normally write. Place the pencil on the middle of the page..."

Go.

Pick something. Tell me what to draw.
Be as detailed as you want.

The Point

AI is the robot.

It does exactly what you say. It doesn't know what you're picturing unless you tell it.

The Framework

The 3 W's

Remember the robot game? You had to be super specific or I drew the wrong thing. The 3 W's give you a cheat code - answer these three questions and your prompt will be clear every single time.

The 3 W's

Ask yourself these before you prompt anything.

W
What
What do you want? Be specific.
"A sneaker website with a homepage, a product page, and a checkout page."
W
Why
What's it for? Who's it for?
"It's for selling custom sneakers to kids my age who love streetwear."
W
Way
How should it look, feel, or work?
"Dark background, bold fonts, big product images, Nike-style layout."

Your Turn

Add a new product
to your website.

You're going to create a brand new product for your website using Lovable. But first - write the prompt using the 3 W's. Don't open Lovable until it's ready.

Step 1
Write it down
Use paper or a notes app. Answer all 3 W's for the new product you want to add.
Step 2
Robot check
Read it back. Would the robot know exactly what to do? If not, add more.

Write your prompt.
Don't open Lovable yet.

Fill this in for your new product:

What: What is the product? What does it do? What does the page look like?

Why: Who is this product for? Why would someone buy it?

Way: How should it look on the site? Colors, layout, images, price, description style?

Take your time. A great prompt saves you time later.

Congratulations.

You should be proud of what you built today. You learned how to think, how to communicate with AI, and how to turn an idea in your head into something real on a screen.
