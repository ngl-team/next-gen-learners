1. What we're building

A custom Customer Relationship Management system for Tectural Construction. The system is owned by Tectural at handoff, not rented. No per-user subscription. No monthly fee to operate.

The build includes:

  • Three pipelines: Roofing, Solar, and Painting / General Construction. Leads route into the correct pipeline at intake.
  • Per-job client profiles. Each job lives at a single record with the property address, photos, documents, saved correspondence, and full history.
  • Live-link client portal. Each customer gets a unique link that opens their own job profile (read-only) so they can see photos, status, and documents without calling.
  • Contract automation with three tiers (basic, medium, complex) and material sub-variants. Selecting a tier and material auto-generates a proposal with pricing and terms.
  • Auto-invoice generation when a job is marked complete.
  • Address autocomplete on lead intake and job creation.

2. Iterative build until acceptance

The headline term. Brayan will continue to refine the CRM at no additional cost until Jamil signs off in writing that the system works the way he wants it to. Acceptance is Jamil's call, not Brayan's.

Refinement is bounded to the functionality listed in Section 1. New features outside that list (in-house AI estimator, custom website rebuild, standalone email assistant, accounting integrations) are scoped and priced as separate engagements.
