Part 1

AI vs Me

You and AI get the same challenge.
You do it first. Then AI tries.
Let's see who wins.

Round 1

Describe your
dream bedroom

Write 3 sentences describing your dream bedroom.
Think about what you'd put in yours:
A slide from your bed to the floor · A mini racetrack around the walls · A trampoline instead of a floor · A secret door behind a bookshelf · A ceiling that looks like outer space · A vending machine with free snacks · A gaming setup with 3 monitors · A rock climbing wall to get to your bed
Example:
"My dream bedroom has a bunk bed with a slide that goes into a ball pit. There's a mini racetrack built into the walls where I can race RC cars. My dog has his own little bed right next to mine with a treat dispenser."

Grab a pen. You have 3 minutes. Go.

Now ask AI
the same thing.

"Describe my dream bedroom in 3 sentences."
Example prompt you could try:
"Describe my dream bedroom in 3 sentences. I love basketball, gaming, and the color blue. Make it sound awesome."

Add your own details. The more YOU you put in, the better it gets.

Round 1 Discussion

Which description feels
more like you?

Your version

Has YOUR personality, YOUR memories, YOUR weird details

AI's version

Polished, detailed, sounds good... but could be anyone's room

Round 2

Invent a new
ice cream flavor

Give it a name, describe what's in it, and draw the cone or cup.
Example:
"Campfire S'mores Crunch - chocolate ice cream with graham cracker pieces, marshmallow swirl, and tiny chocolate chips. Served in a waffle cone dipped in chocolate."

3 minutes. Get creative. Make it yours.

Now ask AI
to invent one too.

"Invent a new ice cream flavor for kids. Give it a fun name and describe what's in it."
Try adding details like:
"Invent a new ice cream flavor for kids. I love peanut butter, cookies, and caramel. Make it over the top and give it a funny name."

Compare yours to AI's. Which one would you actually order?

Round 2 Discussion

Which ice cream would you
actually order?

Your flavor

Based on what YOU actually like. Your favorite candy, your go-to toppings. It's personal.

AI's flavor

Sounds fancy and creative... but does it know what YOU crave on a hot day?

What we learned

What AI does better

  • Makes things look polished
  • Writes fast
  • Gives you lots of options
  • Never runs out of ideas

What YOU do better

  • Knows what matters to you
  • Has real feelings and stories
  • Makes it personal
  • Actually understands the world

AI is a tool. You are the thinker.

Now let's see what happens when
the thinker uses the tool.

Part 2

Remix
Challenge

Everyone starts with the same prompt.
Where you take it is up to you.

Your starting prompt

"A cat in a city"
How to add details:
"A cat in a city... but it's a giant cat the size of a building and people are riding it like a bus"

"A cat in a city... but it's Tokyo at night and the cat is a street food vendor selling ramen"

"A cat in a city... but the whole city is made of cardboard and the cat is the mayor"

Change it. Add to it. Remix it. Go crazy.
The only rule: start from here.
