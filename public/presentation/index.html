Lesson 1 — From Our Danbury Pilot

What AI Is
And What It Isn't

By the end, students believe: "AI is a tool, not a brain — and my thinking matters."

1. Set the Culture
3 norms: no wrong answers, think first, respect ideas
2. Icebreaker
Name, favorite color, dogs or cats — and WHY
3. Mind Map
"Your brain on paper." What you think AI is. No right or wrong.
4. Turn & Talk
Teach your partner 1 thing you know about AI
5. The 4 Truths
AI doesn't think. Doesn't understand. Doesn't know truth. Made by humans.
6. Reflect
"What changed on your mind map? What surprised you?"

This is the exact lesson we ran at the Danbury Library. Full details on the toolkit page.
