How we differentiate

Speed and trust.

Speed: prospects see real software working in their hands within days of the first conversation. They click it, test it, push on it. The artifact does the convincing.

Trust: this is a trust-based business. Superintendents and chiefs of technology buy confidence in the builder before they buy software. Speed of delivery and quality of artifact are how we earn that confidence.

How we close

Leverage. Every district we open names the next one. Roche told me to talk to the chief of technology at Danbury Public Schools. Matt Burns said he would introduce me to Brewster's superintendent. The agency side feeds the EdTech side. We use the smaller builds to sharpen skills, generate tools, and stack proof. Then we walk into superintendents with a working portfolio.
